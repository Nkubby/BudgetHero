🎯 BudgetHero – Your Savings Goal Calculator

Use BudgetHero to find out how long it’ll take to save for anything — whether it's a phone, a trip, or new gear — based on how much you can save weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly.

📌 Example Goals

  • ⚽ Save $300 for a soccer kit by putting away $25/week = 12 weeks
  • 📱 Buy a $900 phone by saving $75 bi-weekly = 12 weeks
  • 🏝️ Take a $1200 trip by saving $100/month = 12 months
